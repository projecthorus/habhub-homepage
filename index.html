habhub

habhub is the home of high altitude ballooning tools developed by UKHAS, and the SondeHub team.
The habitat database was retired in 2022, replaced by the SondeHub database and tracker.

Tools

sondehub-amateur Tracker for amateur high-altitude balloon launches

sondehub Tracker for meteorological radiosondes

predictor Landing predictor written by CUSF

burst calculator Balloon burst calculator written by CUSF

ssdv Live SSDV imagery from HAB payloads

grafana Grafana Dashboards for amateur HAB launches
